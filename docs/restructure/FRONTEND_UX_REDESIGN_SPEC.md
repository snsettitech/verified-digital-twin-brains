## Executive Summary

This specification defines the UX restructure for pivoting from a complex multi-platform Digital Twin product to a focused web-only Clone-for-Experts experience. The redesign organizes the product around three primary areas: **Studio** (Build), **Launch** (Deploy), and **Operate** (Run).
